About BHRC

The Bharatiya Human Rights Council (BHRC) is a non-governmental organization dedicated to protecting and promoting human rights across India. Established with the vision of creating a just and equitable society, we work tirelessly to ensure that every individual's fundamental rights are respected and protected.

Our mission encompasses legal aid, awareness programs, policy advocacy, and direct support to victims of human rights violations. We believe that human rights are universal, indivisible, and inalienable.

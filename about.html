About Bharatiya Human Rights Commission

Dedicated to protecting and promoting human rights in Bharatiya through justice, equality, and community empowerment.

Our Mission

To protect, promote, and ensure the fundamental human rights of all citizens in Bharatiya through effective advocacy, education, and enforcement of justice.

Our Vision

A Bharatiya where every individual enjoys their fundamental rights with dignity, equality, and justice, free from discrimination and oppression.

Who We Are

The Bharatiya Human Rights Commission (BHRC) is an independent statutory body established to safeguard and promote human rights in the state of Bharatiya. We work tirelessly to ensure that every citizen's fundamental rights are protected and upheld.

Since our establishment, we have been at the forefront of human rights advocacy in Bharatiya, addressing violations, providing legal assistance, and creating awareness about fundamental rights among the masses.

Our Core Values

  • Justice: Ensuring fair treatment and equal access to legal remedies
  • Equality: Promoting non-discrimination and equal opportunities
  • Dignity: Upholding the inherent worth of every individual
  • Transparency: Maintaining open and accountable processes
  • Empowerment: Enabling communities to know and exercise their rights

Our Journey

2008 - Foundation

Bharatiya Human Rights Commission was established as an independent statutory body to protect and promote human rights in Bharatiya.

2010 - First Major Case

Successfully resolved our first landmark case involving rights of marginalized communities, setting precedent for future interventions.

2015 - Digital Initiative

Launched online complaint system and digital awareness campaigns to reach more citizens across Bharatiya.

2020 - Community Outreach

Expanded community outreach programs and established district-level human rights cells for better accessibility.

2023 - Modern Platform

Launched this comprehensive digital platform to enhance accessibility and transparency in our services.

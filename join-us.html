Frequently Asked Questions

Applications are typically processed within 7-10 business days after submission.

Can I upgrade my membership later?

Yes, you can upgrade your membership at any time by contacting our membership team.

Is there an age requirement for membership?

Members must be at least 18 years old. We also have youth programs for younger participants.

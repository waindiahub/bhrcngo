Mission & Vision

Our commitment to protecting and promoting human rights in Bihar

Our Mission

To protect, promote and fulfill the human rights of all people in Bihar through effective investigation, advocacy, education and monitoring while ensuring justice, dignity and equality for all.

  • Investigate human rights violations
  • Provide legal aid and support
  • Promote human rights awareness
  • Ensure accountability and justice

Our Vision

A Bihar where every individual enjoys their fundamental human rights with dignity, equality and justice, free from discrimination, violence and oppression.

  • Equal rights for all citizens
  • Discrimination-free society
  • Justice and accountability
  • Peaceful and inclusive Bihar

Our Core Values

The principles that guide our work

Justice

Ensuring fair treatment and equal access to justice for all

Dignity

Respecting the inherent worth and dignity of every human being

Equality

Promoting equal rights and opportunities for all citizens

Protection

Safeguarding human rights and preventing violations

Our Objectives

What we aim to achieve

Conduct thorough investigations into human rights violations and ensure proper inquiry procedures are followed to establish facts and recommend appropriate action.

Promote human rights education and awareness among citizens, government officials, and civil society to create a culture of respect for human rights.

Provide legal aid and support to victims of human rights violations, ensuring access to justice and proper legal representation.

Monitor the implementation of human rights policies and programs, and provide oversight to ensure compliance with national and international human rights standards.

Founding Philosophy

The principles that shaped our institution

Vision

"To create an institution that serves as a beacon of hope for those whose rights have been violated, ensuring that justice is not just a privilege for the few, but a fundamental right for all citizens of Bihar."

"Human rights are not gifts bestowed by governments, but inherent dignities that belong to every person by virtue of their humanity. Our role is to protect, promote, and ensure these rights are respected."
